LezFindr

The latest LGBTQIA+ social network to help connect and create a community for the female, trans, and non-binary members of the LGBTQIA+ community. Totally free and ad supported with unlimited messaging. See the 200 closest users nearby.

  • Client: lezfindr.com
  • Date: 7 March 2024
  • Category: Social, Dating, Networking, Geolocation, Flutter

Key Features For LezFindr App

There was a huge hole in app availability
for the women of the community.

LezFindr was born out of a passion for my community and a need that hadn't received a real working solution. All of my friends would regularly complain that guys had Grindr, Scruff, Jack'd, and many more but everyone else just had filters in the large apps like Tinder and Bumble.

While there is another great app that can help you find your partner, there's nothing that lets you set your intentions for the relationships you foster. LezFindr was created to show you the 200 closest users, let you explore around the world, verify your profile, send unlimited messages, and create your own community.

LezFindr is a community driven app that continues to evolve based on community feedback to make an app that evolves for the userbase as opposed to evolving to earn more. LezFindr is committed to maintaining a free model as we evolve so everyone can connect with those around them. With push notifications enabled, you don't have to constantly be in the app to be seen and to connect.

Procees

Did you know the idea for LezFindr came about 12 years ago? I attempted to hire developers to build an launch the app the first time around. I quickly found that this wasn't the best way to launch an app that has a community driven purpose.

Never giving up on the dream to create this community, as technology evolved I started to learn more about Flutter and started workign to build LezFindr in Flutter. As I continued to teach myself and learn more about Flutter, SQL, Java, Firebase, and tons more technologies, it all started to click into place and make sense.

Continuing development and many development tools, FlutterFlow presented itself as an amazing tool to create and modify a UI while still allowing complete control over code, custom functions, and using as many external packages as needed to create a truly unique app.
